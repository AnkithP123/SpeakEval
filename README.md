## üöÄ **Getting Started**

### **For Teachers**
1. **Sign up** with your institutional Google account
2. **Configure** your first question set with custom rubrics
3. **Create a room** for your students
4. **Share the room code** with your students
5. **Monitor and grade** submissions in real-time

### **For Students**
1. **Receive room code** from your teacher
2. **Join the room** using the provided code
3. **Complete authentication** (if required)
4. **Record your responses** to the speaking prompts
5. **Submit and receive** automated feedback
